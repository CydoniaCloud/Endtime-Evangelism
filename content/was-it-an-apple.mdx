For years we have been taught and believed that Adam and Eve, our first
parents in the Garden of Eden ate an apple from the tree in the midst of
the garden and through this act of disobedience the whole world plunged
into sin.

Why should it be an apple when apples were never mentioned in the Word
of God in reference to the Garden of Eden? Although the Bible speaks
that they were not to eat of the TREE of the knowledge of good and
evil, but never can we find in the entire Scriptures that it was an
apple that has caused our transgressions and fall.

{/* Page 4 — exposition */}

We read in Genesis 2:17, *"The day you eat thereof you'll surely die."*

So this forbidden act introduced death. There was no death, no sickness,
and no old age before this act. There was no hatred, killing or funeral
service before this act of disobedience. There was Perfect Love, Perfect
Life, Perfect Peace and Perfect Joy. This act destroyed the planet.

The first man and woman were created and formed by God, The Creator of
the Universe.

They had no physical father or mother; they never came through sexual
multiplication; they never came forth as babies but came forth fresh
from The Hand of God, as young people.

When God said "Multiply and replenish the earth," did He want them to
reproduce by sexual multiplication? If so, why are we born in sin and
come into the world speaking lies?

## Questions to consider

{/* Page 5 — the questions list, in source order */}

Why is sexual reproduction in the Covenant of Marriage a righteous act
but outside of the Marriage Covenant it is adultery and fornication?

When Adam and Eve committed this act of disobedience, **why did they
cover their nakedness and not their mouths?**

Is child bearing by sexual multiplication God's Perfect Will or His
Permissive Will?

If we were born the right way, **why did Jesus Christ say to Nicodemus,
"You must be born again!"?**

Is AIDS a plague from the original sin?

**After his change from a beast to a reptile, the serpent had to eat
the dust of the earth. Why was this?**

**Why was his structure changed and not his name?**

**Why is dressing, singing, music, dancing, advertising and the movies,
all sexually motivated?**

Why was Adam and Jesus Christ (the second Adam) not sexually born?

Why did David say in Psalms 51:5, *"In sin did my mother conceive me"?*
Was sexual multiplication part of the original Marriage Covenant?

What is the original sin?
